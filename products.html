Footwear

Fly Suede for Comfort & Lifestyle Shoes

Engineered for the growing comfort footwear category — brands producing Birkenstock-style sandals, slip-ons, casual mules, and outdoor-leisure shoes that need both aesthetic softness and material traceability.

Our suede passes Adidas A-01 2024 Full Test (Key Code 100 under Natural Leather classification), giving your sourcing team a recognised benchmark they can immediately work with.

Key characteristics

  • Material composition: Recycled leather & PET fiber
  • Recycled content: 100%
  • pH value: 4.10 (within 3.2–5.5 range)
  • Hexavalent chromium (CrVI): Not detected
  • Formaldehyde: Not detected (<5 mg/kg detection limit)
  • Azo dyes: Not detected (all 24 amines tested)
  • Heavy metals (Cd, Pb, Hg): Not detected
  • SCCP / MCCP chlorinated paraffins: Not detected
  • Organotin compounds (TBT, DBT, etc.): Not detected
  • DMFa (dimethylformamide): Not detected

Test standard

Bags & Accessories

Fly Suede for Fashion Bags & Accessories

For brands and ODM factories producing suede bags, pouches, and accessories at the Coach and Michael Kors quality tier — where surface consistency, color depth, and zero-hazardous-substance compliance are non-negotiable.

Bureau Veritas tested across 23 parameters including PFAS, bisphenols, flame retardants, and quinoline. All items passed.

Key characteristics

  • pH value: 5.10 (within leather "other" range 3.5–7.5)
  • PFAS (PFOS, PFOA, PFHxS, C9-C14 PFCAs): Not detected
  • Bisphenols (BPA, BPS, BPB, BPF): Not detected
  • Formaldehyde: Not detected (<16 mg/kg detection limit)
  • Hexavalent chromium (CrVI, aged): Not detected
  • Azo dyes: Not detected
  • Heavy metals (full panel): Not detected
  • Flame retardants (DecaBDE, HBCDD, etc.): Not detected
  • Chlorinated paraffins SCCP/MCCP: Not detected
  • Organotin compounds: Not detected
  • Quinoline: Not detected
  • Dimethyl fumarate: Not detected
